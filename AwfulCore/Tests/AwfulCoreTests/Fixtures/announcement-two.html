 FIRST OF ALL

Okay, as you can probably assume......... the 2.0 release of XenForo, the one they had been teasing for years now... STILL HAS NOT BEEN RELEASED. No ETA, no nothing. But since these forums are still functioning, we can wait another half a month for them to release the new and improved version for us to upgrade to. I apologize, but nobody there could even provide me with even a rough ETA of release, so I was unable to come back and let you guys know what's going on. If it means anything, they are promising "this year." Take it with a grain of salt.

SECOND OF ALL

Thank you very much to the millennials who have made SA the #7th most popular site for them on the internet! I love you guys!



THIRD OF ALL

While the original donation drive was for the explicit purpose of upgrading the current forums to the new XenForo 2.0 ones, I was originally under the assumption that it would be released sooner, rather than later, because a really shitty fucking thing happens during summer: SUMMER BREAK. Revenue has always plummeted then, and this year was the worst of all. Revenue is around 8% of where it should be at, so some of the donations had to go to pay for some tech work on the forums and to keep everything afloat. NO, I DID NOT WASTE YOUR MONEY ON NON-SA SHIT, I had to use it to keep this place running.
