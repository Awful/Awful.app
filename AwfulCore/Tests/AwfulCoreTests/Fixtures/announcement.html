The Something Awful Forums Store is once again open! The store has been rewritten from scratch to make it easier than ever to buy forums accounts and upgrades. Instead of having to make every single purchase separately, the new store has an exciting new shopping cart feature. As far as I know, we are the first online store to ever have a shopping cart! Congratulations to us!

You can mix and match any combination of items in your shopping cart. You can even apply items to different users in the same cart. Want to buy platinum and archives at the same time you register? You can do it! Want to unban a user in the same cart and buy platinum for him too? You can do that!

To start off, we only have registration and account upgrades enabled. In the coming days we will be enabling the additional items such as custom titles and banner ads. It'll be great.

Thanks for shopping in advance!
